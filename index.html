COVID-19 INFORMATION

IFP will continue to operate during the COVID-19 crisis as long as supplies are available and will be open extra weekends during the months of May through July. However, changes are required for the safety of both volunteers and clients. Food will be distributed in pre-filled bags or boxes rather than self-selected. These will be available for pickup outdoors, weather permitting (otherwise at the door). Please adhere to social distancing guidelines.
